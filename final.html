Conclusions

  • Utah, Minneasota and North Dakota are the top 3 happiest states!
  • While median income explains states that are least happy, it doesn't show any clear correlationfor the states where the happiness rank is high
  • When we adjust the income based on cost of living, it correlates well with some of the happy states. However, the total income is a better indicator for the unhappy states
  • Money cannot completely explain happiness. There are many other factors that need to be accounted for, when calculating the happiness

Discussion

For this project, the data obtained is not for the same year. However, I think the happiness and income rankings are long term trends which means that the data from a span of a couple of years should have a minimum impact on the analysis
